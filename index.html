Tailblazers: Service Dogs Supporting Veterans

We connect veterans with loyal, trainable dogs to help them regain a sense of humanity, safety, and connection as they transition back to civilian life.

Our Mission

Many service members return home carrying invisible wounds. Combat, trauma, and years of training can make everyday civilian life feel distant and unreal.

Tailblazers believes that dogs help veterans reconnect to the part of themselves that feels human, gentle, and safe again.

We aim to support veterans through:

  • Emotional stability and companionship
  • A sense of purpose and routine
  • Trust, connection, and social confidence
  • The journey back into civilian life

Our work grows out of lived experience and love for the dogs who stand by us through everything. This is not just about training tasks—it's about rebuilding a bond with the world.

Every match is made with care, respect, and understanding. Healing is a personal journey, and we honor that every step of the way.

How It Works

1. Reach Out

Veterans or family members can contact us using the form below. Share what you hope a service dog can help with.

2. Assessment

We learn about your needs, lifestyle, and goals so we can match you with the right dog.

3. Matching

We introduce you to a dog that fits your needs. The bond grows naturally and at your pace.

4. Ongoing Support

Tailblazers stays with you — offering guidance and encouragement as you and your dog grow together.
